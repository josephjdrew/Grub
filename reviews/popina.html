Popina.

Budget by the Quay.

Situated behind Sydney’s famous Circular Quay, Popina is a sophisticated dining experience, despite it’s low pricing. The warm lighting, music and ambience of the resturaunt gives off favourable first impressions. When we visited, the restaurant was full to the brim. And as we were to find out, that was with good reason. For $12 each, the degree of individual care and attention given to each of their pizzas was astounding. Unlike many pizzas at this price point, there was a crisp element to the base, given that they are cooked in a wood oven. Fresh produce was abundant in all the dishes. Overall, Popina is well worth the visit.
