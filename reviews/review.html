Green leaf.

One of Cabramatta's hidden secrets...

Nestled away in the back streets of bustling Cabramatta, this sedated and laid back eatery is well hidden. But it is well worth seeking out for. Rice paper rolls, pancakes, noodles and delectable salads, Green Leaf is an absolute treat.
