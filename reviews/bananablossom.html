Banana Blossom.

Seaside Salads...

Settled in the heart of Manly, Banana Blossom offers dishes to cater to any salad lover. With meat based and vegan options available, the fusion of western and eastern flavours go in tandem for fantastic results. Better still, the ability to take one away and sit beside the beach is simply too good to refuse.
