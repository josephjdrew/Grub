A Touch of Europe.

A sweet tooth's haven-

Once dinner is settled, there is little better for the sweet tooth than a visit to the Touch of Europe bakery. Hosting an array of delicious pastries and cakes, the low pricing makes this especially appetising for those on a strict wallet. Family owned and operated, ‘Touch of Europe’ also produces some of the best savoury baked items and bread in the Manly area.
